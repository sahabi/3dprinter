This folder contains the measuremnt data for three experiements:

square.mat 
printing square for 12 layers on an even base. h_b is base, h_s is the final profile.

e.mat 
printing "E" for 12 layers on an even base. h_b is base, h_s is the final profile.

equare.mat 
printing square for 12 layers on an uneven base with 'E'. h_b is base, h_s is the final profile.

value's unit is [mm], grid space is 1/32 [mm].
